AI is taking over. See why it is and how we can stop it.

AI (Or artificial intelligence) is basically a robot that knows almost anything - Even illegal things, with a bit of jiggery-pokery. It can tell you anything, can create anything, and can do it even better than a human. Now, this isn't something to hide in your house about… at least not yet. As of mid-2023, AI LLMs (Large Language Models like ChatGPT) and Robots have not been fused together. But as time goes on, it is almost guaranteed that it will happen, creating dark realities currently only in books like Snow Crash by Neal Stephenson. There are three ways this can go: The dark way, The copilot way, and the anti-ai way. The dark way is that it takes over, and we have to fight for our lives. The copilot way, is that the AI is a copilot for people's jobs, not taking over. The Anti-AI way is that AI is illegal, and eventually becomes an urban tale.

But how do we stop the dark way from happening? Well, there are a few ways. Way 1: People sue ChatGPT and all of the other models, and also sue their creators for fueling a generation of cheating in essays and assessments, and having jobs being replaced by AI. Way 2: We sign a petiton to stop AI. Way 3: Well, we just stop using it making sure there is no reason for AI. Now don't get me wrong, it's good for some things. But for other things, like cheating and replacing jobs - That's where it crosses the line. Join now, before it's too late.
